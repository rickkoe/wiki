Got a Brilliant Idea or a Burning Enhancement Request? Toss it into the IBM Ideas Portal!

Think of the IBM Ideas portal as the suggestion box on steroids. Got a feature request? A tweak that could save hours? Or maybe you’ve uncovered a “Why does it work like this?!” moment? Share it! The portal is where your genius ideas or mild annoyances turn into potential game-changers for IBM products.

Why use it? Because “shouting into the void” doesn’t get updates, but submitting to Ideas just might. Plus, imagine the satisfaction of seeing your idea go from “Wouldn’t it be cool if…” to “Ta-da, here it is!”

So don’t just sit there, unleash your inner innovator—because better tools mean fewer headaches, and fewer headaches mean more time for coffee. ☕
