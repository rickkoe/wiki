Steps are for changing layer by disabling ports on the switch fabrics. 
Purpose for these steps is to ensure the FlashSystem does not see any other systems nodes on the fabric otherwise the system layer will not change. 
* Could also block the ports are the storage level. Ex. satask chfcportfeature -feature port_state -enable no -fcioportid 7
 

  1. (GUI) On FS9200:  Stop partnership to FS5200-DR

Example 

 

  1. (CLI) On FCIP-ROS:  Disable FCIP tunnel to FCIP-NAL

    fabricshow

    portdisable ## 

    fabricshow

  1. (CLI) On FCIP-Temp:  Disable FCIP tunnel to FCIP-ASH

    fabricshow

    portdisable ##

    fabricshow

  1. (CLI) on FS9200:  Change the system layer to replication

    lssystem | grep layer

    chsystem -layer replication

    lssystem | grep layer

  1. (CLI) on FS5200-DR:  Change the system layer to replication

    lssystem | grep layer

    chsystem -layer replication

    lssystem | grep layer

  1. (CLI) On FCIP-ROS:  Enable FCIP tunnel to FCIP-NAL

    fabricshow

    portenable ##

    fabricshow

    switchshow

  1. (CLI) On FCIP-Temp:  Disable FCIP tunnel to FCIP-ASH

    fabricshow

    portenable ##

    fabricshow

    switchshow

  1. (GUI) On FS9200:  Start partnership to FS5200-DR
